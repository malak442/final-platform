About Us

We Make Medical Diagnostics Smarter with Artificial Intelligence

MedVisAI combines the power of medical imaging, large language models (LLMs), and explainable AI to deliver intelligent and transparent diagnostic support. Our platform is designed to assist medical professionals in interpreting X-ray images and automatically generating accurate, natural language reports. We use state-of-the-art deep learning architectures, including CNNs for image understanding and transformer-based LLMs for report generation, enhanced with Grad-CAM for full interpretability. This ensures every insight is not only precise—but also explainable and trustworthy. Whether you're a radiologist, researcher, or student, MedVisAI empowers you with tools to quickly analyze complex data and focus on what matters most: patient care.
